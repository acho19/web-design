Regardless, I am extremely happy to have a starting point for my resume website. Also on the contact folder, I decided to use Formspree because Professor Clayton mentioned how useful it is in class. I am hoping that this website continues to keep a simple style so that potential recruiters can have an easy time accessing my experiences. Please click on either my beautiful face or the navigating header to access my resume.
